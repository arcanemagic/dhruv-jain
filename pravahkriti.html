Pravahkriti | A Social Initiative

Name: Pravahkriti

Duration: July 2018 - April 2019

Skills: HTML, CSS, GML, Javascript

Website: www.pravahkriti.com

Project
Details

Pravahkriti was a social project started in my school aimed at promoting menstrual education and awareness across all sections of society.

Health

Nutritional deficiencies, better lifestyles, exercise, diet.

Hygiene

Access to hygienic products, sanitation facilities.

Awareness

Biological processes, what to do/what not to do.

Positivity

Fighting against socio-cultural taboos, de-stigmatising periods.
