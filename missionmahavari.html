Log: Mission Mahavari

Project Details

Mission Mahavari is a video game I developed to spread awareness about menstrual hygiene and fight against the social taboos surrounding menstruation. This project is aimed towards helping underprivileged girls between the ages of 12-15.

Physical Discomforts

The game shows ways of battling physical discomforts like cramps.

Hygiene

The game shows ways of maintaining hygiene like using sanitary pads.

Household Taboos

Increases awareness about general everyday taboos like being forbidden from washing hair.

Societal Taboos

Taboos like being forbidden from entering places of worship are addressed.
